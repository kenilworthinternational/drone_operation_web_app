Privacy Policy for DSMS Vehicle

Effective Date: January 09, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kenilworth International ("we," "us," or "our") handles information related to the DSMS Vehicle mobile application (the "App"). This App is intended for exclusive use by authorized internal users of Kenilworth International and is not intended for public use.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal data from users. It is designed solely for managing internal operational activities.

Operational Data

The App facilitates the capture of data related to operational activities, in accordance with established company operational procedures. This App includes vehicle data with maintenance schedules and meter readings.

Access and Authentication

Access to the App is restricted to pre-registered internal users of Kenilworth International. User authentication is performed using one-time passwords (OTPs) sent to the user's registered official phone number. We do not store or retain user credentials beyond the authentication process.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data collected or processed by the App with any third parties, except when required by law or legal process.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the operational data captured by the App from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure server hosting

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
